Business Loans

A business loan is an unsecured loan provided to Self Employed Individuals or companies and is generally used for purposes like working capital, business expansion, etc.

Home Loans

Home Loan plays an important role in helping one for easy availability of dream home. There are number of banks or NBFCs that offer home loans for low interest rate.It has few formalities starting from submitting application to disbursement of loan amount.

Cash Credit / Over Draft

Cash Credit is one type of Advance made by Banks to it's Borrowers. By granting this facility Bank allows borrower to withdraw money up to certain fixed amount sanctioned by the Bank.

Car Loans

A car loan is a personal loan that allows the potential buyer to pay the vehicle off in monthly payments instead of having to pay the full price all at once.

Project Finance

Project finance is the long-term financing of infrastructure and industrial projects based upon the projected cash flows of the project rather than the balance sheets of its sponsors. The financing is typically secured by all of the project assets, including the revenue-producing contracts. Project lenders are given a lien on all of these assets and are able to assume control of a project if the project company has difficulties complying with the loan terms.

Loan Against Property

A loan against property (LAP) is exactly what the name implies -- a loan given or disbursed against the mortgage of property. The loan is given as a certain percentage of the property's market value, usually around 60% to 90%.

Working Capital Loans

Working Capital loan is a loan whose purpose is to finance everyday operations of a company.
A working capital loan is not used to buy long term assets or investments. Instead it's used to clear up accounts payable, wages, etc.

Machinery Loans

A Machinery Loan is provided to Individuals or Companies for the purpose of buying and installing new or used machinery and equipment.It's a short term loan with a loan tenure of 6 months to 5 years. It's a secured loan.

Construction Finance

Construction Finance is a loan provided to Builders / Developers to assist them in project acquisition and construction cost.

Reserve Mortgage

A reverse mortgage is a loan available to homeowners who are 62 years or older that enables them to convert part of the equity in their home to cash.
The product was conceived as a means to help retirees with limited income use the accumulated wealth in their homes to cover basic monthly living expenses and pay for health care. However, there is no restriction for how reverse mortgage proceeds can be used.

Personal Loans

A PL is an unsecured loan. It is a smaller loan than a mortgage loan and is generally used to finance a car or other vehicle, renovations to a home ,to finance a vacation of one kind or another and a great number of other things.

Educational Loans

An Educational Loan is a loan offered to students which is used to pay off education-related expenses, such as college tuition, room and board at the university, or textbooks. Many of these loans are offered to students at a lower interest rate.

Commercial Vehicles Loans

A Commercial Vehical Loan is provided to Individuals or Companies to assist in purchasing commercial vehicles like trucks, buses, trailers, etc.A lien is marked on the Commercial Vehicle by the Bank or NBFCs who is funding the loan.
